Why Google Analytics pageviews different from filter by day and by month

2020-01-06 | Hits

I wrote a script to sync Google Analytics (GA) pageviews to own DB in daily basis.
E.g.

  • 2019-12-01 -> 342 pageviews
  • 2019-12-02 -> 621 pageviews
  • 2019-12-03 -> 781 pageviews
  • 2019-12-04 -> 388 pageviews
  • 2019-12-05 -> 562 pageviews
  • ‚Ä¶
  • 2019-12-31 -> 597 pageviews

So that I can generate report (in my web app, not GA) and filter by date range.

But then, I encounter an issue, when I filter the report range from 2019-06-01 to 2019-06-30,
the total pageviews are different from GA report.

Then I cross check it day by day, and both (GA & web app) are tally.

üëÜ from 2019-06-01 to 2019-06-21, both are tally

üëÜ from 2019-06-01 to 2019-06-22, the result are different.
From here, I assume the result for 2019-06-22 has problem

But when I cross check for 2019-06-22, both are tally again ü§î

üëÜ from 2019-06-19 to 2019-06-22, the result are tally also.

Then after googled for a while, I think is caused by the GA sampling, perhaps from range
2019-06-01 to 2019-06-22, the data set are too large, thus Google just pick a sample set of data. See the references üëá
